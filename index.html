FEATURES

HospitalityONLINE has features aimed towards a better patient-doctor relationship. Through real-time note-taking, database linking, and more, the online doctor's visit is being transformed in a way never seen before.

Our team is made up of a singular core developer and ideator and we are actively recruiting volunteers to help push HospitalityONLINE to the next frontier. We've reached out and started partnering with hospitals around Colorado and hope to make a change one visit at a time.
